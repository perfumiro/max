Returns & Refunds

Need to send something back? We keep the process simple, transparent, and stress-free.

How to return

1

Contact us within 14 days

Email ipordiseparfums@gmail.com or reach us on WhatsApp. Include your order number and reason.

2

Get your return label

Our team will send you a return label and instructions within 24 hours of your request.

3

Pack & send the item

Place the item in its original packaging, sealed and unused. Drop it off at the designated courier point.

4

Refund processed

Once received and inspected, your refund is processed within 3–5 business days.

14-day window

Return requests must be submitted within 14 days of the delivery date.

Original condition

Items must be completely unopened, unused, and returned in original packaging with seals intact.

Non-returnable items

Opened perfumes, personalised orders, and gift sets cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

Refund methods

Cash refund

For cash-on-delivery orders, refund is sent via bank transfer within 3–5 business days.

Store credit

Opt for IPORDISE store credit and receive a 10% bonus on top of your refund amount.

Prefer an exchange?

Changed your mind on the scent? You can exchange for a different fragrance instead of a refund. Same conditions apply — item must be sealed and unopened.

No price difference for items of equal or lesser value

Packing tips

Use original box

Seal securely

Add bubble wrap

Attach return label

Questions about your return?

Our support team is available Mon–Sat, 9am–8pm.
