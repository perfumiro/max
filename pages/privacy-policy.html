Privacy Policy

How IPORDISE collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: March 4, 2026

1 Information we collect

We may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, billing details, and order history when you place an order, create an account, or submit a form on our website.

2 How we use your data

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Provide order updates and customer support.
  • Improve website experience and product recommendations.
  • Send newsletters only when you have explicitly subscribed.

3 Information sharing

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties. Information is shared only with trusted service providers — such as delivery and payment partners — when strictly necessary to fulfil your order and operate the service.

4 Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar tools to remember your preferences, improve performance, and understand visitor behaviour. You can control cookie settings at any time through your browser preferences.

5 Data retention & security

We retain personal data only for as long as required for operational, legal, and support purposes. We apply reasonable technical and organisational safeguards — including encrypted storage and access controls — to protect your information from unauthorised access.

6 Your rights

You may at any time request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. You can also unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly.

7 Contact us about privacy

For any privacy-related question or request, contact us at support@ipordise.com or by phone at +212 5 22 00 00 00.

This Privacy Policy was last reviewed on March 4, 2026. Material changes will be communicated via email or a prominent website notice at least 14 days before taking effect.
