Privacy Policy

This page explains how IPORDISE collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you browse our website, place orders, and contact our team.

Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. Information we collect

We may collect information such as your name, email, phone number, shipping address, billing details, and order history when you place an order or submit a form.

2. How we use your data

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Provide order updates and customer support.
  • Improve website experience and product recommendations.
  • Send newsletters only when you choose to subscribe.

3. Information sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information is shared only with trusted service providers (such as delivery and payment partners) when necessary to fulfill your order and operate the service.

4. Cookies & Analyses

Our website may use cookies and similar tools to remember preferences, improve performance, and understand visitor behavior. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data retention & security

We keep personal data only for as long as required for operational, legal, and support purposes. We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information.

6. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, and you can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

7. Contact Us

For any privacy-related request, contact us at support@ipordise.com or by phone at +212 5 22 00 00 00.
