Our Mission

within everyone's reach

Our Journey

2019

The beginning

IPORDISE launched with a carefully hand-picked selection of 50 fragrances from the world's most prestigious houses, shipped from Casablanca.

2021

Expanding horizons

We grew our catalogue to include niche and Arabian oud perfumes, meeting demand for exclusive, hard-to-find scents not available in local stores.

2022

Beauty & wellness

Extended into luxury makeup, skincare, and beauty essentials, becoming a one-stop destination for the conscious beauty lover.

2025

Discovery Sets launch

We introduced our popular Discovery Sets — curated samplers that let customers explore new fragrances before investing in a full bottle.

2026

Today & beyond

Serving hundreds of fragrance lovers across Morocco, with same-day dispatch and a growing community of passionate scent enthusiasts.

Our Values

100% Authentic

Every product is sourced from authorised distributors. We never sell imitations, grey market goods, or tester bottles.

Customer First

Our team is driven by a genuine desire to help you find your perfect fragrance. Your satisfaction is our measure of success.

Responsible Commerce

We partner with ethical suppliers, minimise packaging waste, and are committed to running a responsible luxury business.
