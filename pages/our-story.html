Our Journey

2024

The Beginning

IPORDISE launched in Tangier with a carefully hand-picked selection of premium fragrances from the world's most prestigious houses — making authentic luxury perfume accessible to everyone in Morocco.

2025

Growth & Discovery

We expanded our catalogue to include niche, Arabian oud, and designer exclusives. We also launched our popular Discovery Sets — curated samplers so customers can explore new scents before committing to a full bottle.

2026

Today & Beyond

Serving hundreds of fragrance lovers across all of Morocco, with same-day dispatch, a thriving community of scent enthusiasts, and an ever-growing collection of the world's finest fragrances.
