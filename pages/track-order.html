Troubleshooting

I didn't receive a confirmation

Check your spam folder or WhatsApp messages. If nothing, contact us with your name and order details.

My order is delayed

Rare delays can happen due to peak demand or weather. If your order exceeds 72 hours, contact support immediately.

Wrong order number?

Order numbers start with IPD- followed by the year and a sequence. Example: IPD-2026-00123. Check your email or WhatsApp for the confirmation.
