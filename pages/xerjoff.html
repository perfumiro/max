Italian Haute Parfumerie

XERJOFF

Founded in Turin in 2003 by Sergio Momo, XERJOFF is an Italian luxury house where rare ingredients, Murano glass artistry, and uncompromising craftsmanship converge to create some of the world's most coveted fragrances.

Exclusive Niche Collection

The House

Where Perfumery Meets Art

Every XERJOFF fragrance is a masterpiece born from the marriage of Italy's finest traditions: the art of perfumery, the mastery of Murano glasswork, and an unwavering devotion to the rarest raw materials from every corner of the globe.

From the golden amber of Alexandria II to the timeless warmth of Naxos and the luminous freshness of Erba Pura, each creation tells a story of obsessive craftsmanship, luxury without compromise, and the Italian pursuit of eternal beauty.
