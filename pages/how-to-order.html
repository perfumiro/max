Step-by-Step Guide

Ordering is simple & fast

1

Browse Our Collection

Visit All Perfumes or use the navigation to filter by gender, brand, or mood. You can also use the search bar to find a specific fragrance by name or note.

2

Choose Your Fragrance

Click on any product to open the full product page. Read the fragrance notes, concentration, and customer reviews. Select your preferred bottle size if multiple options are available.

3

Add to Cart

Click the Add to Cart button on the product page. A confirmation will appear. You can continue shopping and add more items, or proceed directly to checkout.

4

Review Your Cart

Open your Cart to review the items, quantities, and total. You can update quantities or remove products before proceeding. When you're ready, click Checkout.

5

Fill In Your Details

On the checkout page, enter your full name, phone number, and delivery address (city & detailed address). Double-check your phone number â€” our delivery team will contact you on this number.

6

Confirm Your Order

Review the order summary one final time, then click Place Order. Payment is Cash on Delivery â€” you pay when your order arrives at your door. No advance payment or card required.

7

Receive Confirmation

You will receive an order confirmation via WhatsApp or SMS with your order number and estimated delivery window. Our team may also call you to confirm the address.

8

Receive & Enjoy

Your order is delivered within 24 to 72 hours across Morocco. Pay the delivery agent the exact order total in cash, inspect your package, and enjoy your luxury fragrance. You can track your order at any time.
